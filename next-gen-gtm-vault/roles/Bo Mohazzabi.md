## Bo's GTM Philosophy

### 1. No Fluff
- Don't say "leverage synergies" — say "use this to close more deals"
- Don't say "thought leadership" — say "write something useful"
- Don't say "alignment" — say "marketing and sales are talking to each other"
- Every piece of communication should be direct, specific, and actionable

### 2. Intent Signals > Spray-and-Pray
- HaystacksAI was built on this thesis: **find the people already showing buying behavior**
- GitHub stars, issues, comments = real intent signals for developer tools
- AI SDRs sending 10,000 generic emails is making the problem worse, not better
- The future: **find the needle in the haystack, then craft one perfect message**

### 3. First GTM Hire Mentality
- At KarmaCheck: did EVERYTHING. Closed deals, built processes, hired team, designed pricing, built PLG funnel
- $36K → $2.4M = **67x growth as a one-person GTM team**
- Series A deck contribution = GTM leader who understands the business, not just the pipeline

### 4. PLG + Sales Hybrid
- Designed subscription pricing model at KarmaCheck (industry first)
- Built self-serve e-commerce product alongside enterprise sales
- This IS [[Product-Led Sales]] in practice
- LTV:CAC of 5:1 proves the hybrid model works

### 5. Practitioner > Consultant
- Not a guy who reads about GTM — a guy who **does** GTM
- 264% quota attainment
- First GTM hire who scaled to $2.4M
- Founded a company, got acquired
