# Product-Market Fit

The market wants your product. Users retain, engage, and tell others. The prerequisite for [[GTM Fit]].

## The Progression

```
Problem-Solution Fit → **Product-Market Fit** → GTM Fit → Growth-Market Fit
```

PMF answers: "Does the market want this product?"
[[GTM Fit]] answers: "Can you sell it repeatably?"

Many startups die with PMF but without GTM Fit — great product, no distribution.

## Signs of PMF

- Users retain without being prompted
- Word-of-mouth drives organic growth
- Users complain when you try to take it away
- Usage grows faster than your ability to support it
- Sean Ellis test: >40% would be "very disappointed" without your product

## PMF ≠ GTM Fit

| PMF | GTM Fit |
|-----|---------|
| People want it | People can find and buy it |
| Validated by retention | Validated by repeatable revenue |
| Can exist without GTM Fit | Cannot exist without PMF |
