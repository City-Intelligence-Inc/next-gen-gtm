# GTM Operating System

A coordinated, AI-driven backbone that connects marketing, sales, customer success, and product into a **single motion**. Not a tool — a system.

## Definition

> A GTM AI Operating System is a coordinated, AI-driven backbone that connects marketing, sales, customer success, and product into a single motion. It is about building a revenue engine that is faster, leaner, and smarter.

## The Three Convergent Capabilities

1. **Unified Data Infrastructure** — All GTM data in one place, accessible cross-functionally
2. **Behavioral Intelligence at Scale** — Real-time understanding of buyer and user behavior
3. **Cross-Functional Orchestration** — Coordinated actions across departments, triggered automatically

## Four Implementation Models

### 1. RevOps as System Architects
- Data-fluent organizations position RevOps to own the entire GTM data layer
- RevOps orchestrates AI enablement across teams
- Works when: RevOps team is technically strong

### 2. Cross-Functional Tiger Teams
- Small "two-pizza" pods: [[GTM Engineer]] + RevOps/DataOps + revenue stakeholder
- Sprint-based approach to building and iterating on GTM systems
- Works when: Fast-moving startup, need to ship quickly

### 3. Hybrid AI + GTM Roles
- New titles: AI Architect, [[GTM Engineer]], AI Workflow Designer, AI Ops Lead
- Dedicated headcount for GTM system design and maintenance
- Works when: GTM complexity warrants specialized roles

### 4. External Agency Partners
- Outsource initial GTM OS development to specialized agencies
- Agency builds the system; internal team operates it
- Works when: No internal technical GTM talent yet

## What a Unified GTM Layer Does

1. **Ingests** — All GTM-relevant data: product events, CRM, marketing, support, community, billing, intent
2. **Resolves Identity** — Single account/contact graph across all touchpoints
3. **Models** — Lead scores, health scores, PQLs, intent signals, ICP fit (transparent, configurable)
4. **Orchestrates** — When signal X fires, trigger action Y in system Z
5. **Activates** — Through any channel: email, LinkedIn, Slack, in-app, phone, chat
6. **Learns** — Feedback loops improve scoring, routing, personalization over time
