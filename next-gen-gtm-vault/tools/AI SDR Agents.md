# AI SDR Agents

AI agents that automate the full outbound SDR workflow: prospecting, research, personalized email writing, sending, and follow-up. Operating 24/7 at a fraction of human cost.

## The Market

- Gartner: 40% of enterprise apps will include task-specific AI agents by end of 2026 (up from <5% in 2025)
- AI agents market projected to exceed **$10.9B in 2026** at 45%+ CAGR
- Average ROI: **171%** from agentic AI deployments (192% for U.S. enterprises)

## Economics

| | Human SDR | AI SDR System |
|---|-----------|--------------|
| Annual cost | $85K-$100K (fully loaded) | $8.4K-$24K |
| Hours/week | 40 (minus admin, meetings) | 168 (24/7) |
| Emails/day | 50-100 | 1,000+ |
| Personalization | High quality, low volume | Moderate quality, high volume |
| Follow-up consistency | Variable | Perfect |

## Hyper-Personalization at Scale

The key unlock: agents can **research + personalize at scale** in ways humans can't:
- Read the prospect's LinkedIn, blog, podcast appearances
- Analyze their company's tech stack, recent news, job postings
- Reference specific pain points relevant to their role and industry
- Boost outbound conversion rates by **up to 7x**

## Limitations & Failure Modes

- **Generic output** — Without good prompts and data, AI emails sound robotic
- **Hallucinated personalization** — AI fabricates facts about the prospect
- **Deliverability risk** — High-volume AI sending can trigger spam filters
- **Brand risk** — A bad AI email reflects on your company
- **Buyer fatigue** — As AI outbound proliferates, response rates may decline industry-wide
- **Compliance** — CAN-SPAM, GDPR requirements still apply
