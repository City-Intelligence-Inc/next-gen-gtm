# AI Agents in GTM

AI agents are becoming **first-class participants** in the GTM process — not just tools, but autonomous workers that research, qualify, personalize, and execute.

## The Market

- **40%** of enterprise apps will include task-specific AI agents by end of 2026 (Gartner), up from <5% in 2025
- AI agents market projected to exceed **$10.9B in 2026** at 45%+ CAGR
- Average ROI: **171%** from agentic AI deployments (3x traditional automation ROI)

## Five Agent Categories

### 1. AI SDR Agents (Outbound)
Automate prospecting, research, personalized email, sending, follow-up.
- **Players:** [[AI SDR Agents|11x (Alice/Jordan)]], Artisan (Ava), AiSDR, Relevance AI
- **Impact:** 24/7 operation, $8-24K/year vs. $85-100K/year for human SDR
- Hyper-personalization boosts conversion **up to 7x**

### 2. AI Inbound Agents
Handle live chat, qualify inbound leads, book meetings, answer questions.
- **Players:** Qualified, Drift (Salesloft), Intercom Fin
- **Impact:** Instant response time, 24/7 availability, consistent qualification

### 3. AI Research Agents
Conduct deep account research, competitive analysis, personalization at scale.
- **Players:** [[Clay]] AI, [[Apollo]] AI, Perplexity integrations
- **Impact:** Minutes of research per account vs. hours for humans

### 4. AI Scoring/Routing Agents
Score leads based on ICP fit and intent, monitor 75+ buying indicators, route to reps.
- **Players:** [[Pocus]], MadKudu, 6sense, custom models
- **Impact:** Real-time scoring instead of batch; transparent logic

### 5. AI Deal Agents
Analyze deal health, surface risks, recommend next actions from conversation data.
- **Players:** Gong, Clari, People.ai
- **Impact:** Automated [[MEDDIC-MEDDPICC]] gap analysis from call transcripts
