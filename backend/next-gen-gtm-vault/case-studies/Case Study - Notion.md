# Case Study: Notion — Template-Driven Viral PLG

**Valuation:** $10B by 2024
**GTM Motion:** [[Product-Led Growth]] + [[Community-Led Growth]]
**Key Stat:** $10B without traditional enterprise sales teams or advertising

## The Playbook

### 1. Product as Canvas
- Notion is **infinitely flexible** — users create custom workflows, wikis, databases, project management
- This flexibility created a **template ecosystem** — the product's most powerful distribution mechanic
- Users don't just use Notion; they **build things in Notion** and share them

### 2. Template Marketplace
- Users create and share templates for every use case
- Templates serve as both **content marketing** and **onboarding**:
  - New user discovers a "Startup OKR Tracker" template
  - Clicks to duplicate → instantly has a working setup
  - Starts using Notion → invites teammates

### 3. Community Ambassadors
- Localized ambassador programs in Japan, LATAM, design/product communities
- Ambassadors create content, host meetups, answer questions
- Word-of-mouth at scale, with zero paid spend

### 4. Organic Traffic Engine
- **95% organic traffic** — built through community content, not advertising
- YouTube creators make "How I Use Notion" videos
- Blog posts, Twitter threads, Reddit discussions
- Template marketplace content is indexed by search engines

### 5. Enterprise Sales Layer
- Added **on top** of PLG once teams exceeded $100/month in spending
- Did **not replace** PLG — augmented it
- Enterprise features: SSO, SCIM, advanced permissions, audit log
- Sales team focuses on expansion, not acquisition

## Why Templates Are Powerful

Templates are uniquely effective because they:

1. **Show value immediately** — New user gets a working setup in 30 seconds
2. **Drive discovery** — Template search is a top-of-funnel acquisition channel
3. **Create creators** — Template makers become evangelists
4. **Build switching costs** — Your workflow lives in Notion; hard to leave
5. **Expand use cases** — Each template = new reason to use the product

## Key Numbers

- **95%** organic traffic
- **$10B** valuation without traditional sales or advertising
- Ambassador programs in **20+** countries
- Thousands of community-created templates

## Key Lessons

1. **Build something people want to customize and share**
2. **Templates > Features for onboarding** — Show, don't tell
3. **Community investment compounds** — Slow to start, powerful once critical mass is reached
4. **Enterprise sales is a layer, not a foundation** — Let PLG prove demand first
5. **The best marketing is user-created content** — Lower cost, higher trust, more authentic
