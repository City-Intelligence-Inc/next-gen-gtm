# Sales-Led Growth (SLG)

A dedicated sales team identifies, qualifies, and converts leads through discovery calls, demos, negotiations, and contract execution.

## When SLG Works Best

- Complex products with **ACV above $25K**
- Multi-stakeholder buying committees (6-10 people)
- Products requiring customization, integration, or compliance review
- Industries with established procurement processes (enterprise, government, healthcare)

## Team Structure

```
SDR/BDR → AE → SE → CSM
   ↓        ↓      ↓      ↓
 Prospect  Qualify  Demo   Retain
```

- **SDR/BDR** — Outbound prospecting, lead qualification
- **AE (Account Executive)** — Discovery, demo, negotiation, close
- **SE (Sales Engineer)** — Technical demo, POC support
- **CSM (Customer Success Manager)** — Onboarding, retention, expansion
- **CRO** — Manages the entire revenue org

## Qualification Frameworks

- [[MEDDIC-MEDDPICC]] — Gold standard for enterprise deals
- **BANT** (Budget, Authority, Need, Timeline) — Simpler, for transactional deals
- **SPICED** (Situation, Pain, Impact, Critical Event, Decision) — Modern alternative

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| Pipeline coverage | 3-4x of quota |
| Win rate | 15-30% (enterprise) |
| Sales cycle | 3-12 months |
| ACV | $25K-$500K+ |
| CAC payback | 12-18 months |

## Strengths

- High deal sizes and control over the sales process
- Relationship-driven loyalty and retention
- Can handle complex procurement and compliance

## Weaknesses

- Expensive to scale (headcount-driven)
- Slow feedback loops
- Bottlenecked by human capacity
- Hard to get right with early / incomplete products

## How AI is Changing SLG

- [[AI Agents in GTM|AI SDRs]] automate prospecting and initial outreach
- [[Signal-Based Selling]] prioritizes the right accounts at the right time
- Conversational intelligence (Gong, Chorus) provides coaching at scale
- [[MEDDIC-MEDDPICC]] qualification can be partially automated by analyzing call transcripts

## Companies Known for SLG

- Palantir, ServiceNow, Snowflake (enterprise tier)
- Traditional enterprise SaaS

## The Hybrid: Sales-Led → Product-Led Sales

Most SLG companies are now adding [[Product-Led Sales]] on top — using product usage signals to inform sales engagement rather than relying solely on outbound prospecting.
