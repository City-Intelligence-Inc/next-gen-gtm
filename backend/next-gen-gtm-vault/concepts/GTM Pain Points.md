# GTM Pain Points

These are the core inefficiencies in how GTM is done today. Each one represents an opportunity for the [[Next-Gen GTM Thesis]].

## 1. Data Silos and Fragmentation

- Average B2B company uses **12-20 GTM tools**, each with its own data model
- Customer data scattered across CRM, marketing automation, product analytics, support, billing
- No single "source of truth" for the customer journey
- **Impact:** Reps see incomplete pictures; teams make decisions on partial data

> Solution direction: [[Warehouse-Native GTM]], [[Identity Resolution]], [[Composable CDP]]

## 2. Manual Workflows and Human Bottlenecks

- SDRs spend hours researching prospects, writing emails, updating CRM records
- RevOps teams manually build reports, clean data, maintain integrations
- Handoffs between Marketing → SDR → AE → CS are lossy and slow
- **Impact:** Human time wasted on automatable work; slow speed-to-lead

> Solution direction: [[AI Agents in GTM]], [[GTM Engineer]], [[Clay]]

## 3. Tool Sprawl and Overlap

- Companies pay for overlapping capabilities across multiple tools
- Average enterprise spends **$1,800/employee/year** on SaaS with significant redundancy (Zylo)
- Each new tool adds integration complexity
- **Impact:** Budget waste; integration hell; cognitive overhead

> Solution direction: [[Composable GTM Stack]], [[GTM Operating System]]

## 4. Stale and Inaccurate Data

- CRM data decays at **~30% per year** (job changes, company changes, email bounces)
- Enrichment is point-in-time, not continuous
- Reps don't trust the data → don't use the tools
- **Impact:** Wasted outreach; missed opportunities; eroded tool adoption

> Solution direction: [[Data Enrichment Waterfall]], [[Clay]], continuous enrichment

## 5. Misaligned Metrics

- Marketing measures **MQLs**; Sales measures **SQLs**; CS measures **NRR**
- No unified revenue metric across the funnel
- Attribution remains unsolved — marketing and sales argue over credit
- **Impact:** Teams optimize for their own metrics, not revenue; finger-pointing culture

> Solution direction: [[Bow-Tie Funnel]], [[GTM Metrics That Matter]], unified revenue architecture

## 6. Slow Feedback Loops

- Weeks or months between campaign launch and understanding pipeline impact
- Product usage data rarely reaches sales teams in real time
- Customer health signals are lagging indicators, not leading
- **Impact:** Can't iterate fast; miss timing windows; reactive instead of proactive

> Solution direction: [[Signal-Based Selling]], [[Event-Driven GTM Architecture]], [[Reverse ETL]]

## The Cost of These Problems

|---------|------|

| Time to PMF (traditional) | 12-24 months |
