# Go-To-Market (GTM)

Go-To-Market is the strategy and operational system a company uses to bring its product to customers. It encompasses **how you find, reach, convert, retain, and expand** your customer base.

## Why GTM Matters

- [[Product-Market Fit]] tells you *what* to build. **GTM Fit** tells you *how to sell it*.
- The best product loses to the better distribution engine.
- In SaaS, the majority of LTV comes from retention and expansion — GTM doesn't stop at "closed/won."

## The Four Fits (Sequential)

1. **Problem-Solution Fit** — Do you solve a real problem?
2. **[[Product-Market Fit]]** — Does the market want your product?
3. **[[GTM Fit]]** — Can you reach and sell to the market repeatably?
4. **Growth-Market Fit** — Can you scale efficiently?

## Core GTM Motions

| Motion | Key Mechanic | Best For |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| [[Sales-Led Growth]] | Dedicated sales team drives conversion | Complex, high-ACV products ($25K+) |
| [[Product-Led Growth]] | Product drives acquisition & conversion | Self-serve, low-ACV products (<$10K) |
| [[Community-Led Growth]] | Users drive awareness & adoption | Dev tools, creative tools, platforms |
| [[Product-Led Sales]] | Hybrid: PLG adoption + sales engagement | Mid-market, PLG → enterprise expansion |
| [[Agent-Led Growth]] | AI agents drive discovery & evaluation | API-first, developer tools (emerging) |

## The Shift Happening Now

The GTM landscape is undergoing a generational shift driven by:

1. **[[AI Agents in GTM]]** — Autonomous SDRs, scoring, research, personalization
2. **[[Signal-Based Selling]]** — Evidence-backed, timing-aware engagement
3. **[[Composable GTM Stack]]** — Best-of-breed components replacing monoliths
4. **[[Warehouse-Native GTM]]** — Data warehouse as source of truth, not CRM
5. **[[GTM Engineer]]** — New role combining technical skills + GTM strategy
6. **[[Agent-Led Growth]]** — AI agents as buyers, not just sellers

> "The winners won't be the ones with the most AI tools — they'll be the ones who design the smartest systems and empower the right people to run them."
