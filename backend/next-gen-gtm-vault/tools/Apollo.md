# Apollo

**Category:** Sales intelligence + engagement (all-in-one GTM platform)
**Position:** The most complete single-platform GTM tool — database + outbound + CRM + AI agents
**Website:** apollo.io

## What It Does

Apollo combines a **275M+ contact database** with built-in email sequences, CRM functionality, and (as of Oct 2025) AI-powered agentic modules.

## Core Capabilities

### Contact Database
- 275M+ verified contacts, 60M+ companies
- Firmographic, technographic, and demographic data
- Real-time data updates and email verification
- Competitive with ZoomInfo at a fraction of the cost

### Sales Engagement
- Email sequences with A/B testing
- LinkedIn task integration
- Call dialer
- Meeting scheduling

### CRM (Lightweight)
- Deal pipeline management
- Activity tracking
- Not as deep as Salesforce, but sufficient for many teams

### AI / "Vibe GTM" (Oct 2025)

Apollo's major bet — four agentic modules:

| Module | What It Does |
|--------|-------------|
| **Outbound Agent** | Autonomous prospecting — finds targets, researches, writes personalized emails, sends, follows up |
| **Inbound Agent** | Qualifies inbound leads, routes to reps, books meetings |
| **Deals Agent** | Monitors deal health, surfaces risks, recommends next actions |
| **Data Enrichment Agent** | Continuously enriches and validates contact data |

Teams define ICP and messaging templates once; AI executes autonomously.

## Positioning

Apollo is the **all-in-one alternative** to the [[Composable GTM Stack]]:

| Composable Stack | Apollo Equivalent |
|-----------------|-------------------|
| [[Clay]] + ZoomInfo | Apollo database + enrichment |
| [[Instantly]] + Lemlist | Apollo sequences |
| Attio / Pipedrive | Apollo CRM |
| 11x / Artisan | Apollo Vibe GTM agents |

**Trade-off:** Less flexibility than composable, but dramatically simpler to operate.

## Who Uses It

- **Startups & SMBs** — All-in-one simplicity, affordable pricing
- **SDR/BDR teams** — Primary daily workflow tool
- **Solo founders** — One tool to do outbound
- **Less common for enterprise** — Salesforce + point solutions still dominate there

## Pricing

Notably affordable vs. competitors:
- Free tier available
- Paid plans start low (vs. ZoomInfo at $15K+/year)
- This aggressive pricing is a key competitive advantage

## Strengths

- All-in-one simplicity — one tool, one login, one data model
- Massive database at low cost
- Vibe GTM agents could be a game-changer if they work well
- Strong API for integrations

## Weaknesses

- Data quality sometimes lags ZoomInfo for enterprise contacts
- CRM is lightweight compared to Salesforce/HubSpot
- All-in-one means you're locked into their ecosystem
- Vibe GTM agents still early — unclear how autonomous they really are
